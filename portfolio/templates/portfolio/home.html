About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate data enthusiast with a journey that started unexpectedly during the COVID-19 lockdown. While stuck in my hometown for two years, I decided to learn Python and quickly fell in love with programming. I spent my time building personal projects, like a COVID-19 Twitter web app that utilized the Twitter API to filter tweets based on user-selected criteria.

From there, I ventured into freelancing, specializing in web scraping. My attention to detail and commitment to delivering quality work led to many repeat clients and exciting projects over the course of a year.

I moved to Melbourne to pursue further studies in Data Science. I completed my course in July 2024. I am currently working as a face-to-face fundraiser, which helped me build confidence, resilience, and excellent communication skills.

Now, I'm excited to bring my passion for data and problem-solving to a team where I can continue learning, growing, and making an impact.
